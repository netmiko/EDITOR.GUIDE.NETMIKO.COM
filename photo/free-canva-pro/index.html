Ways to get Canva Pro for FREE

Now, coming back to the question of how you can get Canva Pro for free, if you want to buy canva pro then it will cost you around 50$ annually or around 6$ monthly, which might not be a lot, but it is a lot if you are a student.

Now, for students, Canva has a special program that is called “Canva for students” which provides students free access to all the pro materials available on Canva like pro elements, pro logos, pro images, etc.

To get Canva for Students you can either create your own Canva for Students account or you can join a Classroom that has Canva for students.

Canva also has a free trial option, which you can use to get 14 Days of free Canva pro account with all the pro features, do note that you will need a valid credit card if you want to get a Free Canva pro trial.

Okay, so now let’s see how you can get a Free Canva Pro by using Canva for students and a Canva free trial.

Canva for Students

To get a canva for students account you will need to first have a .edu email with you, you can get a .edu email from your college/university for free, if your college/university does not provide a .edu email then here are the steps to get Edu email for canva pro –

1. First open your browser and go to https://10minutesemail.net/.

2. Here you will get a temporary .edu email address at the top that you can use for canva. If the email address is not an edu email then click on the delete button on the right side, keep deleting the email addresses until you get an edu email address.

Now copy the edu mail and then go to https://canva.com/education, here click on sign-up.

Enter all the information required like your legal name, the .edu email, and a secure password, once done click on submit. Canva will now send you a verification code on your .edu email to verify the email.

Go back to https://10minutesemail.net/ and there scroll down a little, here in the inbox you will receive the verification code that you need to verify canva, it might take a little bit of time for the email to arrive, once it does, copy the verification code and verify your canva account.

That’s it, your canva for students account is now activated and you can use all the pro features for free.

Now if this method did not work then instead of creating your canva for students’ accounts you can join a classroom created by a teacher where you will have all the features of canva pro. You can comment below and I will send you the link for the classroom.

Canva Free Trial

You can also get Canva pro for free by using the free trial of canva pro, all you need is a valid credit card, remember that you will need to cancel the trial before 14 days otherwise you will get charged.

To get a Canva free trial you will need to first go to https://www.canva.com/pricing/ and here you will see the option to “Try for Free for 14 days” click on it if you haven’t made a canva account yet then make one.

Complete the purchase by entering your credit card and enjoy canva pro for 14 days for free.
